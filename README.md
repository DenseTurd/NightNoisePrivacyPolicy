Privacy Policy – NightNoise

Last updated: 9.12.2025

NightNoise (“the App”) is designed to help users relax and improve sleep by generating ambient and randomized audio. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how your data is handled.

Information We Collect

NightNoise does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The app:

Does not require account registration

Does not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or identifiers

Does not track your location

Does not access your contacts, camera, or microphone

Device Data

NightNoise does not collect analytics, diagnostics, or device data.

All sound generation and processing happens locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

NightNoise does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Internet Usage

NightNoise does not require an internet connection to function.

The app does not send any data to external servers.

Children’s Privacy

NightNoise does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If changes occur, the updated version will be published on this page.
